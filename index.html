ManuFac ERP 3.0 – Comprehensive Manufacturing Solution

A tailor-made ERP system designed specifically for manufacturing enterprises. Integrates production, inventory, accounts, sales, POS, and FBR compliance.

End-to-End Manufacturing Intelligence

From raw material acquisition to finished goods sales — ManuFac ERP 3.0 provides real-time visibility across inventory, production (including scrap & outsourcing), sales, and finance with accurate production costing.
